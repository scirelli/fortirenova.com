What We Offer

Three ways to work with us

Every engagement starts with a single question: where can autonomous intelligence compound your advantage fastest? We build the answer.

Flagship

Agentic Engagements

We design, build, and operate autonomous AI agent systems end-to-end. These are not chatbots or simple automations — they are multi-step reasoning engines that orchestrate tools, delegate to sub-agents, maintain context across sessions, and escalate intelligently when human judgment is required.

What You Get

  • Full-stack agent architecture: planning layer, memory, tool integrations, and observability
  • Evaluation harness and regression testing to prevent capability drift over time
  • Production deployment on your infrastructure or managed by us, with SLA guarantees
  • Continuous iteration loop — agents improve with usage, not just with retraining
  • Dedicated on-call support and runbook documentation for your operations team
Start an Agentic Engagement

Embedded Software Squads

When the bottleneck is raw engineering capacity, we embed senior software engineers directly into your team. No ramp-up theater, no project-manager telephone. Fortirenova engineers own scope, participate in sprint rituals, and ship code alongside your people.

What You Get

  • Hand-picked engineers matched to your stack (distributed systems, data engineering, platform, frontend)
  • Minimum 3-month engagements with an optional extension at the same rate
  • Weekly engineering health reviews identifying tech-debt and risk surface
  • Architecture review and pair-programming sessions with your senior team
  • Full IP assignment — everything we build is yours on day one
Augment Your Team

Advisory & Audits

Before you build, know where to build. Our advisory practice maps your current AI readiness, stress-tests your data architecture, and identifies the three to five opportunities where agentic automation would compound the fastest. Delivered as a structured report and an executable roadmap — not a pitch deck.

What You Get

  • 4-week structured discovery process with key stakeholder interviews
  • Data infrastructure and model readiness audit
  • Prioritized opportunity matrix with effort/impact scoring
  • 12-month execution roadmap with milestone gates
  • 30-day follow-up session to review early implementation decisions

Understand before we build

02 / DESIGN

Architecture before implementation

System design, data contracts, evaluation criteria, and phased milestones. We align on what "done" looks like before anyone touches a keyboard.

Iterative delivery in two-week cycles with a working demo at every checkpoint. Evaluation-driven development means regressions surface on day two, not day ninety.

Systems that compound

Post-launch monitoring, drift detection, retraining pipelines, and on-call support. Agentic systems improve with time when you build the right feedback loops from the start.
